# Fictional Job Ad: Senior Level 2 ASP Electrician

**Company:** VoltStrike Electrical Solutions
**Location:** Alexandria, Sydney NSW (with travel across Greater Sydney)
**Role:** Senior Level 2 ASP Electrician / Electrical Foreman
**Employment Type:** Full-Time, Permanent
**Salary Range:** $120,000 - $145,000 + Super + Fully Maintained Company Vehicle

---

## ABOUT US
At **VoltStrike Electrical Solutions**, we don't just pull cables—we power the future of Sydney. We are a fast-growing, premium electrical contractor specializing in high-end commercial fit-outs, complex industrial upgrades, and advanced smart-grid integrations. We pride ourselves on executing the jobs that other companies put in the "too hard" basket.

Due to explosive growth and newly secured long-term government contracts, we are actively hunting for a highly skilled, no-nonsense Senior Electrician to lead our elite field operations team. We need a problem solver, a leader, and an absolute weapon on the tools.

## THE ROLE
As our Senior Level 2 ASP Electrician, you will be the spearhead of our most critical projects. You won't just be following blueprints; you will be managing sites, diagnosing complex faults, and ensuring our junior sparkies are trained to the highest standard. This is a high-autonomy, high-reward position for an experienced tradie who wants off the standard wages and into a leadership role.

### Key Responsibilities:
- Lead and manage complex commercial and industrial electrical installations from start to finish.
- Perform Level 2 ASP work, including overhead/underground service lines, metering, and live line connections.
- Diagnose, troubleshoot, and repair highly complex, intermittent electrical faults in legacy and modern systems.
- Interpret complex schematics, wiring diagrams, and architectural blueprints to ensure flawless execution.
- Mentor, train, and supervise a crew of up to 4 junior electricians and apprentices, ensuring absolute adherence to safety protocols and AS/NZS 3000 standards.
- Act as the primary technical point of contact for clients, site managers, and general contractors, delivering updates and negotiating on-site variations.

## WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR (The Non-Negotiables)
We are looking for a heavy hitter. If you need your hand held, this isn't the job for you.

- **Experience:** Absolute minimum of 5-7+ years post-apprenticeship experience in commercial/industrial electrical work.
- **Licensing:** Current NSW Electrical Contractor Licence (Qualified Supervisor Certificate preferred) and Level 2 ASP Accreditation.
- **Leadership:** Proven experience managing small teams or leading major projects independently.
- **Technical Chops:** Exceptional fault-finding skills. You must be able to walk onto a chaotic site, identify the core issue, and formulate a safe, efficient repair strategy immediately.
- **Attitude:** A relentless drive for quality, safety, and efficiency. You take ownership of your sites.
- **Bonus Points:** Experience with C-Bus, KNX, or other advanced automation/smart-grid systems.

We demand the best, and we pay for it. 
- **Top-Tier Remuneration:** $120,000 - $145,000 base salary (negotiable based on raw skill and experience) + Superannuation.

- **Growth:** Fast-track progression into a Project Management or Estimating role within 18 months for the right candidate.
- **Culture:** Friday afternoon BBQs, quarterly team events, and a crew that actually works together instead of against each other.
